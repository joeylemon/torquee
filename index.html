How to Use

Drag your mouse across the screen to create a force. Right-click to create a shape.

Use your toolbox to change/erase shapes, zoom in, pan the canvas, etc. You can hold to erase and to pan.

x

Our Project

Torquee™ is all about showing torque in action. It's easy for a new physics student to get confused on how all the components come together to create a torque, so Torquee attempts to help.

Through a simple-to-use interface, students can actively apply forces and see how they create a torque, deepening their understanding of this physics concept.
