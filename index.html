How to Use

You have to drag shapes onto the grid, where you can then apply forces upon the objects using your mouse.

The longer the mouse drag, the larger the force.

x

Our Project

Torquee is all about showing torque in action. It's easy for a new physics student to get confused on how all the components come together to create a torque, so Torquee attempts to help.

Through a simple-to-use interface, students can visualize forces coming together to create a torque and deepen their understanding of this physics concept.
